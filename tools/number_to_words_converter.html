About this converter

Uses the Conway-Guy naming system from "The Book of Numbers" (1995) for precise, standardized number names.

Supports numbers up to 10³⁰⁰³ (one millillion). Accepts scientific notation (3.5e12), expressions (2^64), and bases (0xFF).

Multi-language output for Spanish, French, German, and Portuguese up to one billion.

Shortcuts: Ctrl+C Copy · Ctrl+K Clear · Space Random
